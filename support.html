Ductor Support

Ductor is a GPS-triggered audio guide app for archaeological and heritage sites in Athens and Cyprus.

Contact

For help, questions, or feedback, email support@ductorapp.com.

You can also send feedback directly from the app: Settings → Feedback.

Common Issues

Audio not triggering? Make sure location permissions are set to "Always" or "While Using" in iOS Settings → Ductor.

Narrations not downloading? Check your internet connection and that you have enough storage.

Need a refund? Refunds are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com.
